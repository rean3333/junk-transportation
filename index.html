FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How much does junk removal cost? +

Pricing depends on the volume and type of items. We offer free, no-obligation quotes — call or text us at 602-818-7606 with photos for the fastest estimate. Most single-item pickups start at affordable rates, and full truckloads are competitively priced.

Can I book online? +

Yes! Click "Book Online" anywhere on this page. Fill out your job details, pick a date, and pay a $35 deposit to confirm. We'll follow up within the hour. The balance is due on the day of service.

Do you offer same-day service? +

Yes! Same-day and next-day service is available depending on our schedule. Call us in the morning and we can often be there by afternoon. We offer flexible appointment windows to work around you.

What items do you NOT accept? +

We cannot haul hazardous materials such as chemicals, paint, asbestos, or medical waste. If you're unsure about an item, just call and we'll let you know. We handle just about everything else.

Do you recycle or donate items? +

Absolutely. We donate usable items to local charities and recycle materials whenever possible. We're committed to responsible disposal and keeping as much out of landfills as we can.

How do I get the fastest quote? +

Text photos of what you need removed to 602-818-7606. We can give you an accurate quote within minutes. You can also book online, call, or fill out the form below.
